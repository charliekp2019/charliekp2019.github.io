Travel to Santa Cruz

Santa Cruz is a smallish beach city just a couple hours south of San Fransisco. If you're looking for chill
surf and beach vibes, but hate SoCal, this is the place to be. Santa Cruz holds onto the NorCal vibes and
values we all know and love, but is still a surf destination that values beach culture.



Some fun things to do in Santa Cruz:

  • Surf
  • Go to the Beach Boardwalk
  • Eat at amazing restaurants (you can't go to Santa Cruz without eating at India Joze!)
  • Visit UC Santa Cruz
  • Visit the surrounding farms (pick your own strawberries at Swanton Berry Farms!)
  • Go hiking/camping around the city
  • Drive along Highway 1
  • Walk your dog near the lighthouse

Popular surf spots in/around Santa Cruz:

  • Cowell Beach - crowded, rentals on the beach, good beginner to intermediate surf, family friendly
  • Steamer's Lane - good intermediate to advanced surf, off rocks/cliffs, rentals nearby
  • 3 Mile - 3 miles North of SC, short hike from parking, intermediate to advanced surf
  • 4 Mile - 4 miles North of SC, short hike from parking, intermediate to advanced surf
